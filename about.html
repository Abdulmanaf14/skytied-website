About Sky Tide Logistics

Moving the World Forward

Sky Tide Logistics FZE is a trusted logistics and regulatory solutions provider headquartered in the UAE, delivering seamless freight forwarding, customs clearance, warehousing, and compliance services for businesses across local and international markets.

We specialize in providing end-to-end logistics solutions that simplify supply chains, accelerate market entry, and ensure regulatory compliance. Whether it's moving cargo across borders, registering products with government authorities, or managing specialized medical and industrial shipments, our team is committed to delivering reliable, efficient, and customer-focused solutions.

With a strong network of global partners and extensive knowledge of UAE regulations, Sky Tide Logistics helps businesses navigate complex logistics and compliance requirements with confidence.

Our Story

From Vision to Trusted Logistics Partner

Sky Tide Logistics was founded with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between logistics operations and regulatory compliance. We recognized that businesses entering the UAE and GCC markets often face challenges beyond transportation, including customs procedures, product registrations, and government approvals.

To address these challenges, we built a company that offers more than traditional freight services. We created a comprehensive platform where businesses can manage logistics, regulatory requirements, and market entry processes through a single trusted partner.

Over time, Sky Tide Logistics has expanded its capabilities to support a wide range of industries including healthcare, food & beverage, cosmetics, construction, retail, aviation, and industrial manufacturing. By combining industry expertise, innovative solutions, and a customer-first approach, we continue to help businesses move products efficiently while remaining fully compliant with local regulations.
